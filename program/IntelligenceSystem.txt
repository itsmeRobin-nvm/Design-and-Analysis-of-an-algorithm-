Intelligent System : It is an advanced computer  technology that mimics the human intelligence. The major goal of
Intelligent system is to make decisions,recognise patterns and act exactly like human.
In AI , Intelligent system uses algorithm and data to perform tasks that usually require human intelligence
These systems:
Collect Data 
Analyse the data 
Make decisions 
Take actions
The applications of I.S. are healthcare, industries,robotics and business.
